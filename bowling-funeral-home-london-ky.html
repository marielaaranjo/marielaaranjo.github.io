Traditional Funerals

A traditional funeral service provides a formal and structured way to honor and remember your loved one. It typically includes a visitation or wake, a funeral ceremony, and a graveside service. The visitation allows family and friends to pay their respects and offer condolences, while the funeral ceremony provides an opportunity to celebrate the life of the deceased through eulogies, music, and readings. Finally, the graveside service is where the final farewell takes place.

At Bowling Funeral Home, we understand the importance of personalization. We work closely with you to create a meaningful and personalized traditional funeral service that reflects the unique life and personality of your loved one. From choosing the appropriate music and readings to creating personalized memorial displays, we strive to make the service a true reflection of your loved one's life.

Cremation Services

Cremation services have become increasingly popular in recent years, offering a more flexible and customizable alternative to traditional funerals. Bowling Funeral Home offers a range of cremation options, including direct cremation, memorial services with cremation, and traditional funeral services followed by cremation.

Direct cremation is a simple and cost-effective option where the body is cremated shortly after death, without a formal visitation or funeral service. This option allows for the ashes to be returned to the family promptly. Alternatively, families may choose to have a memorial service with cremation, where the cremation takes place before or after the service. This provides an opportunity for family and friends to gather and celebrate the life of the deceased.

For those who prefer a traditional funeral service followed by cremation, Bowling Funeral Home can accommodate these wishes as well. This option allows for a more traditional ceremony, including visitation, funeral service, and graveside service, followed by the cremation process.

Memorial Services

Memorial services are a meaningful way to remember and celebrate the life of your loved one. Unlike traditional funerals, memorial services do not involve the presence of the deceased's body. Instead, they focus on honoring the memory of the deceased through stories, photos, music, and other personal touches.

At Bowling Funeral Home, we understand the importance of creating a personalized and memorable memorial service.
