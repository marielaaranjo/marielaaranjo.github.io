A consistent skincare routine is essential for achieving flawless baddie skin. Start by cleansing your face twice a day to remove dirt, oil, and impurities. Follow up with a toner to balance your skin's pH levels and a hydrating moisturizer to lock in moisture. Don't forget to apply sunscreen daily to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.
