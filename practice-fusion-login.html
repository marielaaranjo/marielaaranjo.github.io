Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password and regain access to your account.

If you are experiencing login issues due to system compatibility, ensure that you are using a supported browser and that you have the latest version installed. If you have entered incorrect login credentials multiple times, your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons. Wait for a few minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact Practice Fusion support, who can unlock your account and assist you in regaining access.

Two-Factor Authentication Issues

If you have enabled two-factor authentication (2FA) and are experiencing issues receiving the verification code, ensure that you have entered the correct phone number or email address associated with your account.
